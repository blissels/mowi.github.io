MOWI

Short for "Movie World Insights," is a global film recommendation site that offers the best curation from around the world. With a user-friendly interface, MOWI provides in-depth reviews, critical scores, and personalized recommendations based on user preferences. Outstanding features include exclusive content, filmmaker profiles, and exclusive clips. MOWI also builds a community through user reviews and discussion forums. With a focus on diversity and quality, MOWI becomes a discovery hub for global film enthusiasts, bringing cultural richness from every continent to users' fingertips. As an unparalleled portal, MOWI revives the sensation of witnessing new stories and uncovering hidden gems from various countries.


OWI

OWI is the mascot of MOWI, derived from Marathi with the meaning 'Lines in Vedic Verse or Shloka.' OWI is depicted as a red-haired girl with her teddy bear shoulder bag. She has a great passion for watching movies.
